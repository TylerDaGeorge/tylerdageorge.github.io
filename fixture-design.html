Five-Step Design Process

1. Intake

Review part data, machine, current setup, goals, and constraints.

2. Concept

Develop locating, clamping, loading, and chip-control approach.

3. Review

Align with operators, manufacturing engineers, and fixture builders.

4. Detail

Create CAD, drawings, BOM, and setup notes.

5. Support

Support quote, build, revisions, and validation planning.

Typical Scope and Initial Exclusions

Typical scope includes fixture concepts through build-ready mechanical documentation. Initial exclusions: standalone CNC programming, large turnkey automation cells, standalone PLC or electrical controls, machine safety certification, and guaranteed savings before validation.

Industries Served

Industrial equipment, machinery components, fabricated parts, production aids, and other recurring machined work where setup repeatability and spindle time matter.

FAQ

Can Eden build the fixture?

Eden can prepare a vendor quote package when scoped and support builder questions.

Are savings guaranteed?

No. Potential improvements must be validated on the machine with the actual process.

Can you work from imperfect data?

Often. Photos, models, drawings, and machine information help define a useful first review.
