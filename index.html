The Process

Every project moves through a defined series of review, approval, and release stages so decisions stay clear and the final design is ready to manufacture.

  1. Step 01

    Review the part files, machine, production volume, current setup, and the bottleneck to solve.

    Problem
    Review
  2. Step 02

    Develop fixture and automation concepts around access, locating, clamping, and operator use.

    Concept
    Generation
  3. Step 03

    Compare the concepts, choose the best direction, and approve the project scope before detailing.

    Direction
    Approval
  4. Step 04

    Complete detailed CAD, purchased-component selection, tolerances, and manufacturing decisions.

    Finalize
    Design
  5. Step 05

    Review the final design for fit, function, probe or tool access, operator use, and manufacturability.

    Design
    Approval
  6. Step 06

    Release build-ready models, drawings, and component information for fabrication and prove-out.

    Release to
    Manufacturing
  7. Step 07

    Deliver the final engineering package and support the handoff into production.

    Deliver
