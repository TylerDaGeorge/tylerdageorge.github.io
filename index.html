Process

From production problem to usable factory tool.

01

Review the Bottleneck

Review the part, machine, current setup, photos, operator task, cycle requirement, pain point, and production volume.

02

Find the Mechanical Opportunity

Look for repeated indicating, slow loading, poor datum control, manual handling, ergonomic strain, or unnecessary operator effort.

03

Design the Practical Fix

Develop a fixture, tool, mechanism, or process aid around loading, clamping, access, safety, repeatability, and build constraints.

04

Deliver Build-Ready Details

Provide CAD, drawings, purchased component references, setup notes, and vendor-ready documentation when needed.
