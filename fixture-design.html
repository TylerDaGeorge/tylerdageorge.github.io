Custom Fixture Design

Fixtures built around the production problem.

Eden Industrial Systems designs practical fixtures that improve part location, setup speed, clamping access, and repeatability for machining, assembly, and inspection work.

Discuss a Fixture Workholding Design

Best Fit

Use fixture design when the setup repeats or quality depends on location.

  • Long setup or changeover time.
  • Operator-dependent part location.
  • Clamping that blocks tools or inspection.
  • Repeat jobs that need clearer setup discipline.

Problem Statement

Good fixtures make the correct setup easier to repeat.

A fixture should do more than hold a part. It should guide loading, control datums, simplify clamping, protect access, and help operators make consistent decisions across shifts and repeat jobs.

The design work starts with the part, machine, operator, tooling, chips, inspection plan, and production volume. The goal is a buildable fixture that supports real shop-floor use.

Services Included

Fixture support can include:

  • Machining fixture concepts.
  • Assembly and inspection fixture layouts.
  • Locating pins, nests, stops, and datum strategy.
  • Clamp access, tool access, and chip clearance review.
  • Operator loading and setup sequence considerations.

Typical Deliverables

Practical details for review and build.

  • Fixture concept sketches or CAD layouts.
  • 3D CAD models and review images.
  • Vendor-ready drawings and notes.
  • Hardware, clamp, and purchased component references.
  • Implementation notes for setup and use.
