How We Work

A clear path from production problem to usable solution.

Each project is scoped around the outcome you need: faster setup, better repeatability, less operator effort, clearer documentation, or a more scalable process.

01

Discovery

Review the part, process, constraints, current pain points, and target outcome.

02

Concept

Develop practical fixture, tooling, or equipment concepts that fit the production environment.

03

CAD & Review

Create 3D CAD and review the design for access, usability, manufacturability, and safety.

04

Drawings

Prepare manufacturing-ready drawings, documentation, and part details for fabrication.

05

Vendor Support

Coordinate with fabrication or machining vendors when needed to help the design become a real tool.

About

Engineering support grounded in real manufacturing experience.

Eden Industrial Systems provides custom fixture design, manufacturing engineering support, and process improvement solutions for manufacturers seeking to increase throughput, improve quality, and reduce production costs.

The company combines design engineering experience with hands-on machining and manufacturing knowledge to deliver practical solutions that are designed for production, not just CAD. That means considering how a fixture is loaded, adjusted, fabricated, inspected, and used by operators every day.

If your team needs additional engineering bandwidth for a fixture, production aid, custom workstation, or process improvement project, Eden can help turn the problem into a clear, buildable plan.
