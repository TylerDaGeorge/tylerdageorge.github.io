Production Tooling

Tools and production aids designed for real manufacturing constraints.

Eden Industrial Systems designs production tooling, mechanical aids, fixture details, and shop-floor support tools that help teams reduce friction in repeat operations.

Discuss ToolingFixture Design

Best Fit

Use production tooling when repeated manual effort slows output.

  • Operators need a better way to load, hold, move, align, or inspect parts.
  • Production needs a tool that vendors can build.
  • Material handling or staging slows the process.
  • Small mechanical improvements can remove repeated friction.

Problem Statement

Production tooling should make the work easier to repeat.

Useful production tools are often simple: a loading aid, a guide, a stop, a fixture detail, a small workstation improvement, or a material handling aid that removes repeated wasted motion.

The design should fit the operator, the part, the floor space, the build method, and the production goal.

Services Included

Tooling support can include:

  • Production aid and shop tool concepts.
  • Assembly, loading, and handling tools.
  • Fixture details and tool interfaces.
  • Vendor-ready fabrication details.
  • Operator access, safety, and maintainability review.

Typical Deliverables

Practical design output.

  • Revision-ready details for future improvements.
