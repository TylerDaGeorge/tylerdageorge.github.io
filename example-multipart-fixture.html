Review a Similar Setup

Production Problem

A recurring machined part is loaded one at a time, setup requires manual alignment, and repeatability depends heavily on operator technique.

Design Goals

  • Use positive location
  • Load multiple stations
  • Maintain tool access
  • Improve chip clearance
  • Simplify operator loading

Concept Approach

Locating strategy

Use defined datums, hardened stops where appropriate, and repeatable nest geometry.

Clamping strategy

Keep clamps accessible, serviceable, and outside critical toolpaths.

Tool access

Check cutter reach, holder clearance, and maintainability before release.

Chip clearance

Avoid pockets that pack chips and plan washdown or evacuation paths.

Validation steps

Verify fit, toolpaths, first-piece approval, clamp force, repeatability, and operator loading before release.
